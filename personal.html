Hi, my name is Mary. I grew up with my four siblings and a very loud pet bird in Boulder Colorado. I grew up right next to the flatirons so I am an avid hiker and skiier. My childhood consisted of camping in the Rockies and family bike rides along the Colorado River.

I am currently a Junior majoring in Computer Science through the College of Enginnering. I have experience in machine learning, computer vision, data processing, and research. I am an active member of the Society of Women of Engineers as I am one of the girl scout outreach officers. I am passionate about increasing accessibility of STEM opportunities to young girls.

Outside of school, I love watching Bachelor Nation. I also love baking and traveling the world with my family. Music is a big part of my life as I actively played the violin for 15 years.
